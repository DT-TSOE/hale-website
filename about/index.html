About Hale

A Canadian performance marketing agency built for a different kind of growth problem

Hale is a performance marketing agency headquartered in London, Ontario, with team members across Toronto, Calgary, Edmonton, and Vancouver.

We work with consumer, ecommerce, retail, food and beverage, and financial services brands that have outgrown their current marketing engine and need senior people — not junior teams — close to the strategy and execution.

Our services connect strategy, paid media, organic performance, SEO, website development, analytics, and AI search optimization into one growth system, because growth rarely comes from one channel working in isolation.

Our Story

October 2024. A group of career agency leaders decided to do something different.

The agency model was already breaking before most people said it out loud.

Junior teams managed senior clients. Channel specialists operated in silos. Strategy got sold and then handed off. Reporting got busier and less useful. And AI was starting to change what “expertise” meant faster than most agencies could adapt.

Co-founders Matt Bergmann and Michael Perry had spent their careers inside that model — long enough to understand exactly where it was failing the brands it was supposed to help.

Hale was built as the answer to that specific problem: a performance marketing agency where senior people stay close to both the thinking and the execution, where services connect instead of competing for credit, and where the measure of success is whether growth actually moved, not whether the dashboard looked busy.

What We Believe

Marketing should be strategic, measurable, and connected to business growth

  • Senior judgment beats more hands. The right call, made earlier, is worth more than ten people moving in the wrong direction efficiently.

  • Connected execution beats disconnected tactics. Paid, organic, web, email, and analytics working as a system consistently outperform the same channels working independently.

  • Diagnosis before prescription. More budget on a broken system makes the problem more expensive, not smaller.

  • Honest reporting over comfortable reporting. If the data doesn't support the decision, the decision should change, not the data.

What Makes Us Different

Not a bigger team. A closer one.

Most agencies solve growth problems by adding headcount. Senior client leads hand off to junior execution teams. The person who understood the business best stops being the person doing the work.

Hale is built the other way: experienced strategists and practitioners stay close to both strategy and execution throughout. Fewer layers, faster pattern recognition, sharper prioritization. Senior thinkers leveraging technology to move faster and scale.

It's not just our staffing philosophy — it's our business model. Hale is built from the ground up to solve the #1 constraint in most agency relationships: scaling expertise, not hands-on-keyboards.
