Franchise & Multi-Location

One brand, many markets, one system

Multi-location growth has a built-in tension: every location needs to show up locally, while the brand needs to stay consistent everywhere. Handle it location by location and it doesn't scale. Handle it only at the brand level and local visibility suffers.

Halo is how Hale solves that — local search, paid media, and reporting managed across every location from one system, so performance is consistent market to market instead of dependent on whoever's running each one.
