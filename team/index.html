The Team

Senior people, close to the work.

Hale was built on a simple premise: the people who understand the problem should be the people doing the work. That's how this team is structured.

Talk to Hale →See Our Services
Matt Bergmann
Co-Founder

Matt has spent his career building marketing systems for brands that needed to grow and agencies that needed to deliver. As VP of Strategic Partnerships at Arcane, he helped scale one of the fastest-growing agencies in North America before taking on the role of Head of Growth at Republix, where he led business development across a coalition of agencies.

He is a Forbes Communications Council member and contributing writer, a certified Google Ads partner, and a graduate of the Ivey Leadership Program at Western University. Matt co-founded Hale in October 2024 with a clear thesis: the agency model was due for a rebuild, and the brands that needed it most deserved better than what was available.

Michael Perry
Co-Founder

Michael brings expertise across digital strategy, social, analytics, and business development, built across roles at Arcane, Media Mechanics, and SourceStrike. He co-founded Hale alongside Matt Bergmann in October 2024, bringing operational depth and client-side insight to a leadership structure designed around senior people staying close to the work.

His background in digital, social, and analytics keeps Hale's execution grounded in what actually moves business outcomes rather than what looks good in a report.

Lindsay Goodyear
SVP & Managing Director

Lindsay leads the client and delivery side of Hale with a background built entirely in high-ownership, performance-focused agency environments. Her career spans account leadership, DTC ecommerce strategy, and client growth, including years at Arcane where she built deep expertise in what makes brand-agency partnerships actually work.

At Hale, she leads the team responsible for making sure strategy turns into execution and execution turns into measurable results.

Chris Parsons
VP Marketing

Chris brings more than 25 years of retail and marketing leadership to Hale, including tenures as Director of E-Commerce and Marketing at Home Hardware, where he led Canada-wide digital marketing and website development, and Director of E-Commerce at Ren's Pets, where he built the online business from the ground up. Earlier in his career he launched Walmart Canada's ecommerce business and built omnichannel strategy across some of Canada's largest retailers.

He is the founder of Retail Rewired, a retail intelligence platform, and the author of Retail Rewired: How Modern Retail Leaders Drive Growth and Reinvention, published in 2025. He also developed the AIMOD framework, a research-backed report on how AI is changing the way consumers discover brands, built on exclusive data from Caddle in partnership with the Retail Council of Canada.

At Hale, Chris leads marketing and brings the retail brand perspective to client work, helping performance marketing connect to the realities of how shoppers actually discover, consider, and choose.

Dave Thomas (DT)
Sr. Director, Performance Organic & AI

DT has spent more than 15 years in marketing, building his career across digital strategy, content, and growth. At Hale, he leads the organic practice and AI search strategy, currently building out the capabilities, frameworks, and client work that define how the agency approaches non-paid growth.

As a senior marketing strategist, his work spans SEO, content performance, and AI search optimization — helping brands earn visibility that compounds rather than rents attention, and connecting organic activity directly to revenue. His instinct is strategic before it's tactical: find the real constraint first, then build the right system around it.

Jeff Hemphill
Director, Measurement

Jeff is the person at Hale who turns data into decisions that leadership can actually trust. His background runs from business analytics through digital measurement leadership, including nearly seven years at Arcane where he built and led the data and analytics practice, progressing from Manager of Digital Analytics to Director of Data and Analytics.

At Hale, he oversees data collection, integration, and reporting across platforms including Google Ads, Meta, and Shopify, building the measurement frameworks that make performance legible and decisions defensible. His work is the connective tissue between what the marketing is doing and what the business is actually experiencing.

Trevor Carter
Director, Performance Marketing
